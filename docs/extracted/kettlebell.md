## Standard Warm-Up Protocol

Timed protocol, 1–2 min per movement:
- 2 min: Swings both arms
- 2 min: Swings left arm
- 2 min: Swings right arm
- 2 min: Swings alternating arms
- 1 min: Clean left arm
- 1 min: Clean right arm
- 1 min: Push press left arm
- 1 min: Push press right arm

## Key Observations for System

- KB sport RPM targets: Jerk at 10 RPM, Snatch at 14 RPM (1 pood = 16kg)
- Double KB work (2×16kg) is featured in all three cycles
- Exercise progression implied: swing → clean → press → snatch → double KB work
- Timed intervals (1–8 min) are primary format — not set/rep counting
- Goblet squat as accessory to ballistic work
- Farmer's walk as recovery/conditioning between upper-body KB sets
