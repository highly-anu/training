## Philosophy

- Movement quality precedes loading and complexity
- Skill acquisition: isolation → integration → improvisation/spontaneous expression
- Breathing control and joint preparation are non-negotiable foundations
- Daily practice habits (time-based) matter more than session intensity
- Progression: beginner → intermediate → advanced variations within same movement
- Complexity increases via: more complex movement, longer holds, less rest, improvisation added

### Arch Work
- Rotations Into Arch Using Wall: 10 reps
- Slide Into Low Bridge + Rotations Into Arch: combination

### QDR (Quadruped Development)
- Static QDR: 10–30 sec per side, 3–5 sets, 45-sec rest
- QDR Rotational Push-ups (Beginner): 10–14 reps, 60-sec rest
- QDR Rotational Push-ups (Advanced): 14 reps, minimal rest; feet-lifted variant: 10 reps
- QDR Circles: 10 reps per side

### Au Cortado Variations
- Half Au Cortado: 14 reps
- Au Cortado: 10–14 reps
- Au Cortado Presses: 6–10 reps (as slow as possible)
- Au Cortado Press + Rotation Into One Arm Low Bridge: 3 sets of 10/8/6 reps

### Gatherings
- Beginner: 10–12 reps
- Intermediate: 10–12 reps
- Advanced: 6–10 reps

### Corta Capim / Role Work
- Corta Capim Rotations: 15–45 sec per side, 3 sets, 60-sec rest
- Corta Capim Spin: 14 reps, 3 sets, 60-sec rest
- Role work: 2–5 min continuous, 30-sec rest; advanced includes improvisation of learned movements

## Standard Warm-Up Routine

Used across all Floreio sessions:
- 5-min jump rope (or 3 min condensed)
- Wrists Routine (1 set)
- Ido's Squat Clinic Routine (1 set)
- Shoulder ROM and Stabilization Routine (1–2 sets)
- 5 min joint mobility (general)
- Scapula Mobilization (cool-down: 3 sets, 60-sec rest)

## Workout Archetypes

### Floreio Basic (30–40 min)
Structure: Warm-up → Singular Movement Polish (tri-set) → Role Work → Mobility Cool-down

**Example:**
1. Warm-up: jump rope + 4 mobility routines
2. Tri-set: QDR Rotational Push-ups (3×14/60sec) + High Bridge + Au Cortado (3×14/60sec)
3. Role work: 2 min continuous, 3 sets, 30-sec rest
4. Cool-down: shoulder + squat + scapula routines

### Floreio Circuit (No Rest Between Exercises)
- 5-exercise circuit × 3 rounds
- No rest between exercises; 90-sec rest after complete circuit
- Example: Slide Into Low Bridge → Rotations Into Arch → High Bridge Hold → Half Au Cortado → QDR Push-ups

### Floreio Workout of the Day (WOTD, 45–60 min)
1. Warm-up: 5 min + mobility + locomotion patterns (20m each)
2. Equilibre: Handstand Wall Drill (3 sets, 45–60 sec rest)
3. Sequence Work (3 levels):
   - Beginner: Half Au Cortado + Entry Rotation (10/8/6 reps)
   - Intermediate: Au Cortado + Rotation (10/8/8 reps)
   - Advanced: Au Cortado Press + One Arm Bridge (10/8/6 reps)
4. Role Into QDR (10/8/6 reps)
5. Finisher: Bridge/Bridge Rotations + Gatherings (12/10/8 reps), 45-sec rest
6. Prehab: 2 sets Shoulder ROM

### Bridge-Focused Workout
- Equilibre: Static QDR
- Floreio: Head Bridge → QDR
- Conditioning: Shrimp + Hanging Leg Raises + Bridge Push-ups (10/8/6/4/2 descending)

### Explosive Leg Workout
- Jump Into Box Standing: 8 reps, 3 sets, 90-sec rest
- Jump Into Box Iso-Squat: 8 reps, 3 sets, 90-sec rest
- Shrimp (5 reps per leg) + Harop Curl (5 reps): 5 sets, 90-sec rest
- Finisher: 50 single-leg jump rope (calf only) + 60-sec single-leg balance eyes closed

## Equipment
- Open floor space (minimum several meters for locomotion)
- Wall (handstand drills)
- Jump rope
- Optional: box (for jump-into-box work)

## Prerequisite Logic (Implied)
- Low bridge before high bridge
- Basic handstand before handstand walk
- Simple movement mastery before sequences
- Sequences before improvisation
- Isolation → integration → spontaneous expression
